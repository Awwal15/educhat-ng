## 🇳🇬 About the Project

**EduChat NG** is a free, AI-powered tutoring web application designed specifically for Nigerian secondary school students preparing for the **West African Senior School Certificate Examination (WAEC)** and the **National Examination Council (NECO)** examinations.

This project was built as part of the **3MTT NextGen Knowledge Showcase** — a national innovation challenge by the Federal Government of Nigeria's **3 Million Technical Talent (3MTT) Programme**, under the **Education Pillar**.

The application gives every student — regardless of location or income — access to a personal AI tutor, available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, at near-zero data cost.

---

## 🔴 The Problem

Nigeria has over **10 million secondary school students**, yet in rural and underserved communities:

- The average **teacher-to-student ratio exceeds 1:50** in public schools
- Students who struggle with a concept have **no one to ask** after school hours
- **Private tutoring is unaffordable** for most low-income families
- **Poor internet access** blocks students from mainstream online learning platforms
- Millions of students **silently fall behind** in critical WAEC and NECO subjects

The result? Students fail not because they lack ability — but because they lack access.

---

## ✅ The Solution

EduChat NG puts a **patient, knowledgeable, always-available AI tutor** in the hands of every student with a basic smartphone.

Students simply:
1. Open the app — no download, no registration required
2. Select their subject
3. Ask any question in plain English
4. Receive an instant, simplified, curriculum-aligned explanation

Every response uses **familiar Nigerian names, local examples, and WAEC/NECO-specific guidance** — making explanations feel personal and relatable, not generic.

## 🔄 How It Works

```
Student opens EduChat NG
        │
        ▼
  Selects a Subject
  (e.g. Mathematics)
        │
        ▼
  Types their question
  "Explain simultaneous equations with an example"
        │
        ▼
  AI processes the question
  (aligned to WAEC/NECO syllabus + Nigerian context)
        │
        ▼
  Student receives a clear,
  simple, curriculum-aligned explanation
  with Nigerian examples (₦, local names, local scenarios)
        │
        ▼
  Student can ask follow-up questions
  or take a quiz to test understanding
```

## 🤖 AI Disclosure

EduChat NG is powered by an **AI language model** integrated through Lovable.dev.

The AI has been configured with a custom system prompt that:
- Aligns all responses to the **WAEC and NECO curriculum**
- Uses **Nigerian names, examples, and cultural references**
- Keeps explanations **simple and accessible** for SS1–SS3 students
- Provides **exam-specific tips** (paper sections, mark schemes, common errors)
- Redirects off-topic questions back to academic subjects

The AI does **not** store student conversations or collect personal data.

## 🚀 Scalability & Roadmap

EduChat NG is designed to grow with Nigeria's education needs:

- [ ] **Local Language Support** — Hausa, Yoruba, and Igbo interfaces
- [ ] **Voice Explanations** — Audio responses for students with reading challenges
- [ ] **Offline Caching** — Frequently accessed topics available without active internet
- [ ] **Past Questions Mode** — WAEC/NECO past questions with AI-guided solutions
- [ ] **Teacher Dashboard** — Educators can track student questions and learning gaps
- [ ] **JSS Support** — Expand coverage to Junior Secondary School subjects
- [ ] **Progress Tracking** — Student performance analytics over time

Because EduChat NG is web-based and requires no installation, it can scale to every state in Nigeria instantly — reaching millions of students with nothing more than a link and a basic smartphone.
